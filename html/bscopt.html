B.Sc.Computer Science(Optional)

About BSc Computer Science

Running for a period of 3 years, BSc in Computer Science as optional paper, is one of the most popular degree courses after 12th Science. It opens up avenues in various sectors including those specialising in hardware or software technology. There are a number of globally reputed colleges that offer this course and they all share a basic approach towards it.
