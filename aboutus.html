Department of Computer Science

The department of Computer Science was established in the 1993 with computer science subject as one of the Optional subject at B.Sc. level. We have three air conditioned computer laboratories, which are furnished. In order to link with recent knowledge we have the facility of Internet connection for the staff and students. From June 1999 our college conducting post graduate course in computer science, for the post graduate students, there is a department library having number of reference books. We provide facility of reading room for P.G. students of computer science subject. Number of our student developed ‘Application Software’ as academic projects and these application software’s are now implemented in different organization such as Hospitals, Travel agencies and for bank Pigmy agents etc. This computer science department runs some short term courses for college students in order to provide basic computer knowledge. Department has also conducted one month course for non teaching staff to use computer facility in their daily work. College received the permission from university and Govt. Of Maharashtra to start B.C.A. (Bachelor of Computer Application) and B.Sc.(Computer Science) courses from June 2003. We also started skill devolpment program under community college that is Advance Diploma in Software Devolpment in 2019.
